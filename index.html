Startup

Picture.ly (co-founder)

Picturely was a photo search tool that allowed you to "see anything, anywhere in the world". I was drawn to create Picturely because I was fascinated by how technology was changing the traditional media landscape. Crowdsourcing content meant that we could source more angles and greater diversity in a real-time nature that traditional media could not provide.

Through Picturely, I experienced moments that ranged from the powerful (Mumbai bombings) to the downright bizarre (planking). It also taught me the important distinction between a sustainable business and a fun project.

Employment History


Director of Operations: StartupHouse

Over the course of last year I took a derelict warehouse in SoMA and transformed it into the world's largest co-living space for entrepreneurs. We have a 4 1/2 star rating and have built a community numbering 300 entrepreneurs strong. Culture was an important focus from the beginning so StartupHouse has whiteboards installed throughout the building, a 40ft spinnaker hanging in the kitchen, art murals and an inspiration wall for essays.

Knowledge Worker: Capgemini, Accelerated Solutions Environment

Knowledge Workers help to deliver events that are designed to initiate or accelerate change through high-touch facilitation and design thinking methodologies. Engagements typically involve large scale clients who are engaged in rapid organizational change.

Knowledge workers are the Swiss Army knives of an innovation space and the range of duties can include digital recording and production (film & photography), DJing, spatial design, photography, graphic facilitation and group facilitation. Whiteboards were heavily relied upon to assist in the rapid iterative process.
